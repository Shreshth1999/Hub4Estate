- Multi-brand product catalog (Havells, Polycab, Schneider, Finolex, L&T, and 50+ major brands)

- Industry knowledge hub and community features
- CRM and inquiry pipeline management

**Market Opportunity:**
India's construction industry is projected to reach $1.4 trillion by 2025. The building materials segment alone represents ₹50,000+ crore, with less than 5% currently transacted digitally. The shift to digital procurement is inevitable, and Hub4Estate is positioned to capture this transformation.
