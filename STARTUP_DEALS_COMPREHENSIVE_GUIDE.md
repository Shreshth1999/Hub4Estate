## STRATEGY: HOW TO MAXIMIZE YOUR CREDITS

### Phase 1: Foundation (Day 1-30)
1. Apply to **AWS Activate** (Founders tier for immediate $1K)
2. Apply to **Google for Startups Cloud Program**
3. Apply to **Microsoft Founders Hub** (no funding required)
4. Set up **Cloudflare** free plan
5. Apply to **GitHub for Startups**
6. Set up **Clerk** free tier (50K users) or apply to **Auth0 for Startups**

### Phase 2: Growth Stack (Day 30-90)
7. Apply to **Notion for Startups** (6 months free)
8. Apply to **HubSpot for Startups** (90% off)
9. Apply to **Mixpanel** or **Amplitude** startup programs
10. Apply to **Intercom Early Stage** (90% off Year 1)
11. Set up **PostHog** free tier
12. Apply to **Sentry for Startups**

### Phase 3: Scale (Day 90+)
13. Upgrade AWS/GCP credits via VC partners (Portfolio tier)
14. Apply to **Datadog for Startups** ($100K)
15. Apply to **NVIDIA Inception** (if AI startup)
16. Apply to **Stripe Atlas** (if incorporating in US)
17. Apply to **Salesforce Launchpad** (when CRM needed)

### Pro Tips
- **Stack credits**: Many programs can be combined (e.g., AWS + GCP + Azure)
- **Use aggregators**: Check Secret, FounderPass, and F6S before paying retail
- **VC partnerships unlock higher tiers**: Ask your investors about portfolio perks
- **Apply early**: Many programs have limited slots or require being "new" to the product
- **Track expiration dates**: Most credits expire in 12-24 months
