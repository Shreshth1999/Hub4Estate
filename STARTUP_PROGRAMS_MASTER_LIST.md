### 1. DPIIT Startup India Recognition

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Organizing Body** | Department for Promotion of Industry & Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce |
| **What They Offer** | Official "recognized startup" status enabling: 3-year tax holiday (Section 80-IAC), angel tax exemption, self-certification under labor/environment laws, fast-tracked patent filing (80% fee rebate), access to government tenders, eligibility for all Startup India schemes |
| **Funding Amount** | No direct funding -- this is a recognition/certification that unlocks ALL other government schemes |
| **LLP Eligibility** | YES -- LLPs are explicitly eligible. Entity must be incorporated as Pvt Ltd, LLP, or Registered Partnership |
| **Other Eligibility** | Less than 10 years old; annual turnover under Rs. 100 Crore; working on innovation/improvement of products/processes/services; not formed by splitting an existing business |
| **How to Apply** | Register at https://www.startupindia.gov.in -> Apply for DPIIT Recognition -> Submit Certificate of Incorporation + pitch deck/writeup on innovation |
| **URL** | https://www.startupindia.gov.in/content/sih/en/startup-scheme.html |
| **Deadline** | Rolling/Always open |
| **Difficulty** | EASY -- Approval typically in 2-7 working days. Free of charge. No agents needed |
| **Priority for Hub4Estate** | CRITICAL -- APPLY IMMEDIATELY. This is the gateway to everything else |

### 2. Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Organizing Body** | DPIIT, Government of India (via empaneled incubators) |
| **What They Offer** | Up to Rs. 20 Lakh as grant for proof of concept/prototype/product trials. Up to Rs. 50 Lakh as investment (convertible debentures/debt) for market entry, commercialization, scaling |
| **Funding Amount** | Rs. 20 Lakh (grant) to Rs. 50 Lakh (investment). Total scheme outlay Rs. 945 Crore across 300 incubators |
| **LLP Eligibility** | YES -- Private Limited Company, Registered Partnership Firm, or LLP can apply |
| **Other Eligibility** | Must have DPIIT recognition; less than 2 years old at time of applying to incubator; at least 51% shareholding by Indian citizens; must have viable business idea with market fit |
| **How to Apply** | Apply through empaneled incubators (not directly). Go to https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in -> Select "For Startups" -> Login with Startup India credentials -> Choose an empaneled incubator near you |
| **URL** | https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in/ |
| **Deadline** | Rolling -- depends on individual incubator timelines |
| **Difficulty** | MEDIUM -- Need DPIIT recognition first, then apply through incubator. Funds disbursed in milestone-based installments |
| **Priority for Hub4Estate** | HIGH -- Rs. 20L grant is non-dilutive. Apply through Startup Oasis Jaipur or other Rajasthan incubators |

## CRITICAL NOTE: LLP vs. PVT LTD

**HUB4ESTATE is currently an LLP.** This is important because:

- **Government Schemes**: Most accept LLPs (DPIIT, SISFS, iStart, CGTMSE, Mudra, MeitY)
- **Cloud Credits**: All accept LLPs
- **Student Programs**: Individual -- entity type irrelevant
- **VC/Angel Investment**: Almost ALL require Private Limited Company for equity investment. LLP does not have "shares" for equity dilution
