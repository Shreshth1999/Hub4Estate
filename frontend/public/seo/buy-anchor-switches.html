About Anchor by Panasonic

Anchor Electricals is one of India's oldest and most trusted electrical brands, founded in 1963 in Mumbai. Acquired by Panasonic Corporation (Japan) in 2007, Anchor now operates as Anchor by Panasonic — combining six decades of Indian market understanding with Japanese engineering standards and quality control.

Anchor's Roma series is arguably the most widely installed switch in Indian residential construction. The brand offers a complete range from budget (Roma) to premium (Rider, Ziva) to luxury (Penta), along with wires, MCBs, and LED lighting. Anchor switches are known for their durability, wide dealer network, and competitive pricing — making them the default choice for cost-effective projects.
