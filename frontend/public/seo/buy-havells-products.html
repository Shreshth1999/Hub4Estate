About Havells India Limited

Havells India Limited is one of India's largest and most trusted electrical products companies, founded in 1958 and headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh. With annual revenue exceeding Rs 18,000 crore, Havells is a household name synonymous with quality, safety, and reliability across the Indian electrical market.

From the iconic Lifeline Plus FRLS wires to the premium Crabtree switch range, Havells commands dominant market share in wires, switchgear, lighting, and fans. The company owns several popular sub-brands including Crabtree, Standard Electricals, and REO — each catering to different price segments and use cases.
