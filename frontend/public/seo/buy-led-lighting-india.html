LED Buying Guide

For Homes

Living room: 15-18W panel or downlights. Bedroom: 9-12W warm white. Kitchen: 18-20W tube light. Bathroom: 9W moisture-resistant. Use Philips for best color rendering, Syska for value.

For Offices & Commercial

Open offices: 2x2 LED panels (36W or 40W). Cabins: 15W recessed panels. Corridors: 5-7W downlights. Meeting rooms: dimmable panels. Use Philips or Wipro for warranty.

For Construction Projects

Bulk order 100+ units for best pricing on Hub4Estate. Common specs: 12W and 15W panels for apartments, 18W tubes for parking, 50W floodlights for exteriors.
