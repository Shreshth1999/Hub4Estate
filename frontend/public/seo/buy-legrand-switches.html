About Legrand India

Legrand is a global leader in electrical and digital building infrastructure, headquartered in Limoges, France, with a strong presence in India since the 1990s. The company is the world's number one specialist in electrical installations and information networks, operating in over 90 countries.

In India, Legrand is best known for its premium switch ranges — Mylinc (value), Myrius (mid-range), Arteor (premium), and Lyncus (luxury smart). Beyond switches, Legrand manufactures MCBs, distribution boards, cable management systems, and smart home solutions. The brand is especially popular among architects and interior designers for its clean aesthetics and modular design system.
