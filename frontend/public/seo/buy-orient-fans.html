About Orient Electric

Orient Electric Limited is one of India's leading consumer electrical companies, part of the CK Birla Group — one of India's largest diversified business houses. With a legacy spanning over 60 years and annual revenue exceeding Rs 2,800 crore, Orient Electric is a trusted name in fans, lighting, home appliances, and switchgear.

Orient is India's second-largest ceiling fan manufacturer and a pioneer in energy-efficient BLDC technology — they were among the first to launch inverter-technology ceiling fans in the Indian market. The Aeroquiet series is renowned for its whisper-quiet operation, while the Energy Star range leads in BEE-rated energy efficiency. Beyond fans, Orient has a strong presence in LED lighting, water heaters, and air coolers.
