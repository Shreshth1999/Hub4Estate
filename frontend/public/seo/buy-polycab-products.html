About Polycab India

Polycab India Limited is India's largest wire and cable manufacturer by revenue, founded in 1996 and headquartered in Mumbai. With annual revenue exceeding Rs 14,000 crore, Polycab commands the number one position in the organized wires and cables segment with over 22% market share.

Polycab started as a pure wires and cables company and has since expanded into switches, LEDs, fans, and solar products under the FMEG (Fast Moving Electrical Goods) category. Their wire range — particularly the Optima, Etira, and Eco series — is the go-to choice for contractors and electricians across India due to consistent quality and competitive pricing.
