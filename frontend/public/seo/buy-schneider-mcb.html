About Schneider Electric

Schneider Electric SE is a French multinational and global leader in energy management and industrial automation, operating in over 100 countries with annual revenue exceeding EUR 36 billion. In India, Schneider Electric has been present for over two decades and is the preferred brand for electrical distribution, protection, and automation in commercial and premium residential projects.

Schneider's Acti9 range of MCBs is considered the gold standard in circuit protection worldwide — trusted by electrical engineers, consultants, and contractors for its superior breaking capacity, precision tripping, and build quality. Beyond switchgear, Schneider offers premium switch ranges (Zencelo, Aether, Livia), contactors, variable frequency drives, and complete panel solutions.
