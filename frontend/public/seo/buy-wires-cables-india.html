Wire Buying Guide — How to Choose

For House Wiring

Use FRLS (Fire Retardant Low Smoke) wire — mandatory for residential buildings. Sizes: 1.5mm² for lighting, 2.5mm² for power sockets, 4mm² for AC/geyser, 6mm² for main supply. Top picks: Havells Lifeline Plus, Polycab FRLF, Finolex FR PVC.

For Commercial Buildings

Use FRLS wire with higher gauge. 4mm² for general circuits, 6mm² for heavy loads, 10mm² or 16mm² for main distribution. Consider armoured cables for outdoor/underground runs.
