Why Your Fan Choice Matters More Than You Think

Ceiling fans run for 12-16 hours daily in most Indian homes, making them one of the largest consumers of residential electricity. A typical 75W regular fan running 14 hours daily costs Rs 3,800/year at Rs 8/unit. A 28W BLDC fan doing the same costs Rs 1,430/year — saving Rs 2,370 per fan, per year. With 3-5 fans per home, that is Rs 7,000-12,000 in annual savings.

BEE Star Rating for Fans

Since January 2022, BEE star rating is mandatory for ceiling fans in India. The rating is based on the Service Value (SV) — a ratio of air delivery to power consumption.
