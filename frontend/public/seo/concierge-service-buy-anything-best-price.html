Concierge vs Other Options

Hours of calling sellers, no way to verify prices or dealers, inconsistent quotes, and you handle all logistics yourself. The "free" option that costs you the most in time.

Retail prices with 5-15% seller commission baked in. No bulk support, no negotiation, and counterfeit risk in electrical categories.

Why the Concierge Exists

Hub4Estate was founded on a simple observation: the same product gets quoted at wildly different prices by different dealers. A speaker at ₹1,05,000 through one channel costs ₹68,000 through another. The difference is not quality — it is access.

Most people do not have time to track 8 dealers for a single product. They should not have to. The concierge gives everyone access to the best dealer networks, regardless of their connections, location, or buying power.

This is not a communication problem — it is an access problem. And the concierge solves it.
