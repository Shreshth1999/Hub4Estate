Dealer Registration Process

  1. Step 1: Apply Online — Fill in your business details: company name, GST number, product categories, brands stocked, delivery zones.
  2. Step 2: Verification — Our team verifies your GST registration, trade license, business address, and brand authorization. This typically takes 2-3 business days.
  3. Step 3: Catalog Setup — Upload your product catalog (manual entry or CSV import). Set pricing, delivery terms, and inventory levels.
  4. Step 4: Go Live — Once verified, your dealer profile goes live. You start receiving matched buyer leads immediately.
  5. Step 5: Grow — Respond to buyer inquiries, win bids, and watch your revenue grow.
