About Havells India Limited

Havells India Limited is one of India's largest and most trusted electrical products companies, founded in 1958 and headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh. With annual revenue exceeding Rs 18,000 crore, Havells is a household name synonymous with quality and reliability in the Indian electrical market.

Havells manufactures and markets a comprehensive range of electrical products including wires and cables, switches and switchgear, lighting fixtures, fans, water heaters, and home appliances. The company owns several popular sub-brands including Crabtree, Standard, REO, and Reo.
