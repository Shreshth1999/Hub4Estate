The Hidden Cost of Amazon's Commission

Why Amazon Is Not Built for Electrical Procurement

Amazon is a retail marketplace. It excels at one-click purchases of consumer goods. But electrical procurement has different needs:

  • Bulk quantities: Amazon limits order quantities and does not negotiate per-unit pricing
  • Brand authenticity: Electrical products need verified sourcing — counterfeit switches, cables, or panels are a safety hazard
  • Delivery coordination: 200 LED panels or 500m of cable need coordinated logistics, not courier delivery
  • Expert guidance: Choosing the right specs for wiring, panels, or switches requires domain knowledge Amazon cannot provide

When Amazon Makes Sense

For buying 1-2 retail electrical items with fast delivery, Amazon is convenient. But the moment you need more than 10 units, need to compare dealer prices, or want someone to source the best deal — Hub4Estate is the better choice.
