Why IndiaMART Falls Short for Electrical Products

IndiaMART is a directory, not a procurement platform. It connects you with sellers, but the actual buying process — comparing prices, verifying dealers, negotiating, and coordinating delivery — is entirely on you.

For someone buying 200 LED panels or 500 metres of cable, that means calling 6-10 suppliers, comparing quotes in a spreadsheet, and hoping the cheapest one is actually reliable. Hub4Estate eliminates all of that.
