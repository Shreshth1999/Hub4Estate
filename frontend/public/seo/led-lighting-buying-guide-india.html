Why LED Lighting?

LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting has revolutionized the Indian lighting market. LEDs consume 75-80% less energy than incandescent bulbs, 40-50% less than CFLs, and last 15,000-50,000 hours compared to 1,000 hours for incandescent bulbs. India's shift to LED lighting saves over Rs 18,000 crore annually in electricity costs.

With dozens of LED brands and hundreds of products in the Indian market, choosing the right LED can be confusing. This guide breaks down everything you need to know.

Types of LED Lights

1. LED Bulbs

The most common LED product. Direct replacement for incandescent and CFL bulbs. Available in B22 (bayonet) and E27 (screw) bases. Wattage ranges from 5W to 50W.

  • Best for: General room lighting, table lamps, outdoor fixtures
  • Popular wattages: 9W (replaces 60W incandescent), 12W (replaces 80W), 15W (replaces 100W)
  • Price range: Rs 50 - Rs 300 per bulb

2. LED Panel Lights

Flat, uniform-light panels that sit flush with the ceiling. Available in round and square shapes. Surface-mounted or recessed. The most popular choice for modern Indian homes.

  • Best for: Living rooms, bedrooms, offices, false ceilings
  • Popular wattages: 6W, 9W, 12W, 15W, 18W, 22W
  • Price range: Rs 150 - Rs 800 per panel

3. LED Tube Lights (Battens)

Long, linear lights that replace traditional fluorescent tube lights. Available in 2ft (10W) and 4ft (18-22W) sizes. Can be surface-mounted or integrated with existing tube light fixtures.

  • Best for: Kitchens, garages, warehouses, commercial spaces
  • Price range: Rs 150 - Rs 500 per tube

4. LED Downlights

Recessed ceiling lights that create focused, directional lighting. Available in various beam angles (30 to 120 degrees). Used for accent lighting and task lighting.

  • Best for: False ceilings, accent lighting, display areas
  • Price range: Rs 200 - Rs 1,500 per unit

5. LED Strip Lights

Flexible strips with LED chips that can be cut and adhered to surfaces. Used for decorative and ambient lighting. Available in single-colour and RGB variants.

  • Best for: Cove lighting, under-cabinet lighting, decorative accents
  • Price range: Rs 200 - Rs 1,500 per 5m roll

6. LED Floodlights

High-power lights for outdoor and industrial use. Available in 10W to 500W. IP65 rated for water and dust resistance.

  • Best for: Outdoor areas, building facades, sports facilities, security lighting
  • Price range: Rs 300 - Rs 15,000 per unit

Understanding LED Specifications

Lumens (Brightness)

Lumens measure actual light output, not watts. Higher lumens = brighter light. When buying LEDs, compare lumens, not watts.

Colour Temperature (Kelvin)

Colour temperature defines the warmth or coolness of the light.

  • Warm White (2700-3000K): Yellowish, cozy light. Best for bedrooms, living rooms, restaurants.
  • Neutral White (4000-4500K): Natural daylight feel. Best for kitchens, bathrooms, offices.
  • Cool White / Cool Daylight (5000-6500K): Bright, bluish-white. Best for garages, workshops, commercial spaces. Most popular in India.

CRI (Colour Rendering Index)

CRI measures how accurately a light source renders colours compared to natural light. Scale of 0-100, where 100 is perfect. Look for CRI above 80 for residential use, above 90 for retail and display lighting.

Beam Angle

Narrow beam (15-30 degrees) creates focused spotlight effect. Wide beam (90-120 degrees) creates general ambient lighting. Most LED bulbs and panels have wide beam angles.

Room-by-Room LED Guide

Bedroom

2-3 warm white or neutral white LED panels (12W each) for general lighting. Optional: LED strip for cove lighting, 1-2 downlights for accent. Total: 2,000-3,000 lumens.

Living Room

3-5 LED panels (15-18W) or a combination of panels and downlights. Use warm white for cozy feel or neutral white for versatile use. Total: 3,000-5,000 lumens.

Kitchen

2-3 LED panels or battens (18-22W). Use cool white or neutral white for task visibility. Under-cabinet LED strip for counter lighting. Total: 3,000-4,000 lumens.

Bathroom

1-2 LED panels (12-15W) neutral or cool white. Must be moisture-rated for wet areas. Total: 1,000-2,000 lumens.
