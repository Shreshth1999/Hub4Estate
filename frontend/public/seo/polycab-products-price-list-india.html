About Polycab India Limited

Polycab India Limited is India's largest manufacturer of wires and cables with over 50 years of heritage. Founded in 1996 and headquartered in Mumbai, Polycab has annual revenue exceeding Rs 14,000 crore and commands over 22% market share in the organized wires and cables segment. The company operates 25+ manufacturing facilities across Gujarat and Maharashtra.
