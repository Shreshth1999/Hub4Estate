The Problem
3 calls minimum.
Still no honest price.

Buying electrical materials in India is chaos. Every project, every purchase — the same broken workflow repeats.

❌ No Price Transparency
Same cable. 6 dealers. 6 different prices.
FRLS 2.5mm² wire: ₹83/m to ₹127/m across 6 dealers in the same city. No benchmark exists. No way to know who's fair.
❌ WhatsApp + PDF Chaos
15 chats. Outdated catalogues. No comparison possible.
Buyers juggle WhatsApp messages, voice notes, and screenshots. No central source. Everything is scattered.
❌ Quality Dealers Are Invisible
Trusted suppliers have zero digital presence.
Discovered only through word-of-mouth. Buyers default to nearest — not best value. Overpayment is normalized.
